I am Er. Rabi Adhikari, a registered Civil Engineer currently based in Kathmandu, Nepal. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering from Purwanchal Campus, Tribhuvan University, graduating First Division with 71.54%.

Currently employed at Hydrocon Nepal Pvt. Ltd. (Feb 2023 – Present), I have contributed significantly to survey and infrastructure projects across Nepal — from road DPR packages spanning 180 km to UAV and LiDAR-based topographic surveys for bridges, hospitals, and government buildings.

I am licensed under the Nepal Engineering Council, Civil "A" Category (Reg. No. 31893), equipped with deep expertise in Total Station, DGPS, drone surveying, structural design, and DPR preparation for ADB and government-funded projects.
